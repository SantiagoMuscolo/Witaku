Portgas D Ace

Portgas D. Ace (ポートガス・D・エース Pōtogasu Dī Ēsu?), born Gol D. Ace (ゴール・D・エース Gōru D. Ēsu?), nicknamed Ace "Fire Fist" (火拳のエース Hiken no Ēsu?),
was the sworn older brother of Luffy and Sabo, and the biological son of the late Pirate King Gol D. Roger and Portgas D.
Rouge. Ace was adopted by Monkey D. Garp, as Roger himself had requested before his execution.
He was also the commander of the 2nd division of the Whitebeard Pirates and a former captain of the Spade Pirates.
