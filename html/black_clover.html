What is black clover?

Black Clover is a manga series written and illustrated by Yuki Tabata. The series is published in Shueisha's weekly Shonen Jump magazine.
The story revolves around a young man named Asta, born without magical power,
something unknown in the world in which he lives

Zenon Zogratis

Zenon Zogratis 「ゼノン・ゾグラティス, Zenon Zoguratisu」 is one of the Zogratis brothers and a member of the Dark Triad,
a group of 3 powerful mages who currently rule the Kingdom of the Spade.
He is the host of the demon Beelzebub, one of the rulers of the underworld
